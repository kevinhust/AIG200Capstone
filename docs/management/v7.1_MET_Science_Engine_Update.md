**Release Date:** 2026-03-12

## Overview

v7.1 transforms Health Butler from an "experience-based advisor" to a "scientifically-grounded fitness coach" by integrating the **MET (Metabolic Equivalent of Task)** Science Engine based on the Compendium of Physical Activities.

## Three-Tier Decision Priority

The FitnessAgent uses a three-tier priority system for recommendations:

| Priority | Tier | Rule |
|----------|------|------|
| 1 | **Safety First** | Health conditions avoid contraindicated movements; recent heavy meals reduce intensity |
| 2 | **Budget Matching** | Remaining > 300 kcal → High; 100-300 → Moderate; < 100 → Low |
| 3 | **Scenario Intelligence** | Home → Bodyweight; Gym → Full equipment; Limited time → HIIT |
